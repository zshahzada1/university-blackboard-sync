## AI Writing Avoidance Rules (apply to every piece of new text in this plan)

Per Wikipedia's "Signs of AI Writing" guide, the new text must not use:

**Banned words/phrases:** additionally, align with, bolstered, crucial, delve, emphasising, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight, interplay, intricate, key, landscape, meticulous, pivotal, showcase, tapestry, testament, underscore, valuable, vibrant, groundbreaking, renowned

**Tone target:** Direct, occasionally blunt, first-person where appropriate. Conclusions stated plainly without hedging padding.

import re

def delete_ref_para(doc, unique_text):
    """Remove the complete <w:p>...</w:p> containing unique_text."""
    # Match the paragraph opening tag up to and including the closing </w:p>
    pattern = re.compile(r'<w:p\b[^>]*>(?:(?!</w:p>).)*?' + re.escape(unique_text) + r'.*?</w:p>', re.DOTALL)
    matches = list(pattern.finditer(doc))
    assert len(matches) == 1, f"Expected 1 match for '{unique_text}', got {len(matches)}"
    return doc[:matches[0].start()] + doc[matches[0].end():]

doc = delete_ref_para(doc, "Ben Mehrez, I., Farhat, A. and Ajina, A. (2025)")
print("Ben Mehrez deleted")
